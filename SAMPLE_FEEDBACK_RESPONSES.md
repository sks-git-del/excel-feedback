## 📈 Quick Rating Reference

### Rating Scale
```
1 = Poor (Not satisfied)
2 = Fair (Needs improvement)
3 = Good (Satisfactory)
4 = Very Good (Above average)
5 = Excellent (Outstanding)
```

### Pattern Examples

**Excellent Faculty** (4.5+ average):
- Most questions: 5
- Some questions: 4
- All high ratings

**Good Faculty** (3.8+ average):
- Mix of 4 and 3
- Few 5s
- Positive comments

**Average Faculty** (3.0-3.5 average):
- Mix of 3 and 2
- Few 4s
- Suggestions for improvement

**Needs Improvement** (<3.0 average):
- Many 2s and 1s
- Few 3s
- Critical feedback
